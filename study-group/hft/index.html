Syllabus

Week 1: Introduction to HFT

High Frequency Trading Fundamentals

  • Story: The motives and history of HFT.
  • Review: Handling time-series financial data.
  • Market Microstructure: Introduction to the order book (Tick data).
  • Participants: Understanding Market makers & Market takers.
  • Practice: EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) on tick data.

Week 2: Introduction to Machine Learning

Building the Foundation

  • An overview of Machine Learning concepts.
  • Data preprocessing and cleaning techniques.
  • Feature Engineering: Extracting signals from raw data.
  • Modeling: Using regression models as a baseline.
  • Model evaluation and basic optimization.

Week 3: Advanced ML Techniques

Refining the Models

  • Preprocessing: Standardization and normalization methods.
  • Regularization: Modeling penalty terms (constraints) using Ridge and Lasso to prevent overfitting.
  • Optimization Process: Implementing Cross-Validation (CV).

Week 4: Gradient Descent & Boosting

State-of-the-Art Algorithms

  • Introduction to numerical optimization and Gradient Descent.
  • GBM (Gradient Boosting Machine) Models:
    • LightGBM
    • CatBoost
    • XGBoost

Week 5: GPU Acceleration & Tuning

Performance Optimization

  • Introduction to Google Colab and GPU computing.
  • Parameter Searching:
    • Grid Search
    • Bayesian Optimization methods.

Week 6: Discussion

  • Group discussion on strategies and project review.
